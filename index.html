About Me

Hello! I'm Adrijeet, a cybersecurity enthusiast and professional passionate about creating secure systems that protect digital assets and information. My journey in information security began during my undergraduate studies at Vellore Institute of Technology, where I specialized in Information Security.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's in Information Systems Security at Concordia University, deepening my knowledge in advanced security concepts and techniques. My research interests include blockchain technology, biometric authentication, and secure voting systems.

Throughout my career, I've worked on innovative security solutions, from blockchain-based e-voting systems to keystroke dynamic authentication mechanisms. I believe in the power of technology to create more secure and trustworthy digital experiences.
